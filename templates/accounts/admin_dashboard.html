Register

Register the users through this section. Enter the basic details like name, username, and password to register the user. Once registration is done, add images of the users and then train the whole dataset.

2
Add Images

Add Images of the users through this section. The video feed will open to capture the images of the user and will close automatically after capturing 30 images. It take few seconds for a single user dataset.

3
Train

Train dataset of all the users in one go. Be patient and don't refresh in between. After complete training, a new window will automatically pop up showing the results. It takes approximately 1 minute for a dataset of three users.
