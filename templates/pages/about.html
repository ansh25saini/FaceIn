Welcome to Face In

FaceIn is a one in all attendance system that makes use of face recognition to take attendance. The major features of the system are its accuracy, instant email facility and, advanced analytics.

The accuracy of this touchless attendance model is more than 99%. Besides this, after marking the attendance, a mail is automatically sent to the user about the same. The system also allows the user to see the attendance analytics through both normal and graphical representation.
